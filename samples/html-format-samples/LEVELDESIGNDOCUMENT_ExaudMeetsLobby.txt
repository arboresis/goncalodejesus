<b>Requirements</b>
☛ Be adaptable as a virtual meeting venue, and a conference/team-building activity lobby
☛ Customizable for client events (i.e. possibility to include branded elements)
☛ Hold up to 100 concurrent users

<b>Proposed Design</b>
☛ Common area for generalized interactions
☛ Private areas where the noise from the common area is dimmed

<hr style="margin-block: 0; border-top: 2px solid #00000018;" />
